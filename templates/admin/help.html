🆘 Help & Support

Everything you need to run your CRM as an admin

📋 Frequently Asked Questions

➕

How do I onboard a new customer?

Use Create New Customer on your dashboard to set up their login and business profile in one step.

👁️

How do I view a customer's details?

Click View next to any customer in your dashboard list to see their business profile, documents, and history.

📊

How does lead conversion work?

Open Leads, then click Convert on a lead to turn it into a full customer account.

📄

How do I upload a document for a customer?

From a customer's profile page, use Upload Document to attach bills, dues, or reports on their behalf.

🕓

Where can I see activity history?

Visit History to track every action you and your customers have taken across the platform.

🔒

How do I update my profile or password?

Go to Profile to update your contact details, photo, or change your password securely.

Still need help?

Reach out to the system administrator for further assistance.
